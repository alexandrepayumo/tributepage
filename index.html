benigno aquino iii

the 15th filipino president

Here's a timeline of Benigno Aquino III's life:

  • 1960 - Born in Manila, Philippines
  • 1980 - His father was released from prison and allowed to go to the US
  • 1981 - Graduated from Ateneo de Manila University with a degree in economics
  • 1983 - His father returned to the Philippines and was assassinated
  • 1986 - Became vice president of his family's Best Security Agency Corporation
  • 1993 - Began working for his family's sugar refinery company
  • 1998 - Became a member of the Liberal Party
  • 2006 - Became vice chairman of the liberal party
  • 2007 - Made a successful bid for a senate seat
  • 2009 - Announced his candidacy for the 2010 presidential race
  • 2010 - Won the presidency by a wide margin
  • 2012 - Lead the conclusion of a peace agreement with the Moro Islamic Liberation Front
  • 2021 - Died at the age of 61

You can see more information about Benigno on his Britannica entry
